What we collect

If you agree to participate in this evaluation, we will automatically track your location, accelerometer, device-generated activity recognition, app usage time and battery usage. We will also allow you to optionally enter your experiences and feedback during weekly surveys. Furthermore, there will be an initial survey upon first launch of the application to collect information about your age, socioeconomic status, and health status. This information will never be made public or sold, and is collected directly by the CEO without commercial third party involvement. We use an open source platform, e-mission, for the data collection. You can inspect the data that e-mission collects in the background at https://github.com/e-mission/e-mission-data-collection.git, view the configuration for this pilot at https://github.com/e-mission/e-mission-phone/tree/ceo_ebike_project, and see the automated analysis that we perform at https://github.com/e-mission/e-mission-server/tree/ceo_ebike_project

Confidentiality

Your study data will be handled as confidentially as possible. If the results of this study are published or presented, individual names and other personally identifiable information will not be used. To minimize risks to confidentiality, we will store study data on a secure, encrypted E-Mission platform server. The server can only be accessed by analysts associated with the pilot.
