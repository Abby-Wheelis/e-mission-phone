ELCITA and CiSTUP: Electronics City Travel Behavior Study

Introduction and Purpose

Thank you for installing e-mission. e-mission will collect data on your location and speed to make inferences about your trip such as mode choice. Please keep e-mission on your phone for at least two days. At the end of two days, you are requested to pull up your trips and answer questions to confirm inferences made by e-mission.
Please turn on your location services while traveling. The application automatically turns on GPS only when you are moving, so it will not drain your battery.
