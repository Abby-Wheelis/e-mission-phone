ELCITA and CiSTUP: Electronics City Travel Behaviour Study

This version of E-Mission is designed to record your travel behavior for a period of two week days or preferably longer, in order to know about your travel patterns along with that of other commuters to and residents of Electronics City. The application will collect trip location data, travel speed, and estimate mode of travel such as walking, car etc. All data collected is used solely for the purpose of research.
